Day 1: Arrive in Delhi and visit the Red Fort, Qutub Minar, and Lotus Temple.

Day 2: Explore Delhi further, including India Gate, Humayun's Tomb and Chandni Chowk.

Day 3: Drive to Agra and visit the Taj Mahal, Agra Fort, and Itmad-ud-Daula.

Day 4: Drive to Jaipur and visit Amber Fort, City Palace, and Jantar Mantar.

Day 5: Explore Jaipur further, including Hawa Mahal, Jal Mahal and Birla Temple.

Day 6: Drive to Jodhpur and visit Mehrangarh Fort, Jaswant Thada Memorial, and the Umaid Bhawan Palace.

Day 7: Drive to Udaipur and visit the City Palace, Jagdish Temple, and the Monsoon Palace.

Day 8: Take a boat ride on Lake Pichola and visit Jagmandir Island Palace and the Sajjangarh Wildlife Sanctuary.

Day 9: Drive to Jaisalmer and explore the Golden Fort, Patwon Ki Haveli, and Gadisar Lake.

Day 10: Drive to Bikaner and visit the Junagarh Fort and the Lalgarh Palace.

Day 11: Drive to Mandawa and visit the Mandawa Fort, Hanuman Prasad Goenka Haveli and the Murmuria Haveli.

Day 12: Drive to Jaipur and visit the Nahargarh Fort, the Albert Hall Museum, and the Bapu Bazaar.

Day 13: Drive to Sariska National Park and explore the wildlife.

Day 14: Drive to Pushkar and visit the Pushkar Lake and the Brahma Temple.

Day 15: Drive back to Delhi for your departure flight.
