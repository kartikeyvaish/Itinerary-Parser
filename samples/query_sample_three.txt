On the very 1st day I want to Arrive in Delhi and visit the Red Fort, Qutub Minar, and Lotus Temple.
Also explore clubs and bars in Delhi.
Take a rickshaw ride through the Old Delhi streets.

Explore Delhi further, including India Gate, Humayun's Tomb and Chandni Chowk.

On the 3rd day, I want to Drive to Agra and visit the Taj Mahal, Agra Fort, and Itmad-ud-Daula.

On the fourth day, Drive to Jaipur and visit Amber Fort, City Palace, and Jantar Mantar.

Day 5: Explore Jaipur further, including Hawa Mahal, Jal Mahal and Birla Temple.

Drive to Jodhpur and visit Mehrangarh Fort, Jaswant Thada Memorial, and the Umaid Bhawan Palace.

Drive to Udaipur and visit the City Palace, Jagdish Temple, and the Monsoon Palace.

Take a boat ride on Lake Pichola and visit Jagmandir Island Palace and the Sajjangarh Wildlife Sanctuary.

Drive to Jaisalmer and explore the Golden Fort, Patwon Ki Haveli, and Gadisar Lake.

Drive to Bikaner and visit the Junagarh Fort and the Lalgarh Palace.

Drive to Mandawa and visit the Mandawa Fort, Hanuman Prasad Goenka Haveli and the Murmuria Haveli.

Drive to Jaipur and visit the Nahargarh Fort, the Albert Hall Museum, and the Bapu Bazaar.

Drive to Sariska National Park and explore the wildlife.

Drive to Pushkar and visit the Pushkar Lake and the Brahma Temple.

Drive back to Delhi for your departure flight.
